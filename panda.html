Accomodation and Assimilation (2020)

3D animation, 1min 7sec (no sound)

Beauty Standard / Identity / Panda / Popularity


3D animation “Assimilation and Accommodation, A Story of Bear” created by MAYA is the story about the transformation of a human-shaped bear into a human-shaped panda.

In some Asian countries, pandas have been treated as a cute, peaceful, and popular animal while bears have an aggressive, strong, and violent impression. I consider being the same as the others, people sometimes call being “normal,” would be the easiest way to live.

But I hate living in the way that the other people define I should be. Also, in some Asian countries, having lighter skin is one kind of beauty standards. There are some countries that even have skin bleach.
In contrast, however, you can view the story in a positive way. To give a neutral mood to the audience, I tried not to set the mood with facial expression, music, or colors. If you do not like how you look like, you can step out and partially change who you are, keeping your identity inside, just like people do makeup.

In this story, putting white paint is the way to kill your uniqueness resembling the background. In other words, being the same color as the environment makes you transparent. Putting black paint is the way to have a different identity by caring for who you are expected by the others as well as hiding who actually you are. You could consider the white paint as water that washes who you are right now.
