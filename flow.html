Flow (2019)

Digital Video

Flow is your mind state that forgets all feelings and the concept of time.
Studying psychology, I found that flow would be the happiest moment human could have: no emotion and no time.
I used a sound piece called “flow” I made a year ago and made a video piece based on that.
Considering the idea of flow, this piece represents relativeness of time, using the shape of hour glass.
