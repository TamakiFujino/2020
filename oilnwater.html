Oil and Water (2018)

Digital Video

This piece is made based on the story I had in the I left Japan to study abroad.
This piece mainly about generation and cultural conflict, talking about myself planning to get a tattoo of favorite flower of my grandmother.
But also, this is about the unexplained emotions that comes from a conflict between my grandmother who experienced World War 2
and I who chose to live in the United States and under the huge influence here.

Soap and water color represent strong wills to break up the border between oil and water and create the ink to draw a tattoo.
