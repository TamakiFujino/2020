Poop Ribbon (2019)

Installation on the wall of stairhall with ribbons

Slacktivism / Awareness Ribbon

This project starts with the critisizm towards awareness ribbon made by a comedian, George Denis Patrick Carlin. He critisized awarenessas as one of the form of "slacktivism." The term was created by combining slacker and activism. It is a term to explain superfacial activities that do not require money, time, and effort. Carlin even stated that the people who just put awareness ribbons should have brown-colored ribbon, indicating the color of poop, instead. As an artist, we often deal with social issue as the subject of work. Without a deep understanding of the issue, we would just use the issue just as the subject; it is almost a fascinating prey to artists. I have seen some of artwork taking a social issue got a lot of criticizm even though the artist is working on the issue with a strong passion. This piece was installed in the Art Building of San Jose State Univeristy. I would like to see the future artists to be away from slacktivism because art has such a big power to change the world.

Instruction
(1) Pick one poop ribbon(or more if you want!) See the list of awareness ribbon.

(2) Remove the paperbase inside and straighten your ribbon. Please put your paper base into plastic bag below.

(3) Research about your ribbon color and write down what you did not know, you want to remember, or you want to tell to other people.

(4) Hang your ribbon! You can put your name if you want to. You can hang your own ribbon.

List of Ribbon Awareness
Pink: Breast Cancer

Blue: Freedom Speech, Press, and Association Online / Parkinson Disease

Red: HIV and AIDS / Substance abuse

Purple: Domestic Violence / Homophobia

Orange: Multiple Sclerosis / Leukemia

White: Ending Male Violence against Women and Girls

Yellow: Suicide / Millitaly Force / Endometriosis

Green: Cerebral Palsy / Mental Health / Kidney Disease

Examples
Yellow Ribon - Suicide awareness
(1) Basic Information "Suicide is the most common over age of 70"

(2) Numbers "828,000 (1.5% worldwide in 2015)"

(3) Your Experience "I sometime feel like I do not wanna feel anything"

(4) Your Opinion "Suicide of an entire family is the saddest thing in aging societies"

(5) Artpiece Related to The Topic "The Suicide By Edward Manet"

(6) Finding After Research "US is the only country that gun-related sucide is over non-gun-related"

(7) Incident/Related Issue "Jonestown (mass suicide in 1978) - 909 death"

(8) Useful Link/Resource "Call:1-800-273-8255"
