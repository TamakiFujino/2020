Dialogue (2018)

Digital Video, 3min 47sec

Digital Generation / Screen / Debate / Speak Up

She focused on the shift of form of “dialogue” of people in modern society. Ever since every family got a TV at their house, the information we get and give is more virtual.

As a result, digital literacy, knowledge of how to use digital information is a key to being a part of a society and speak up there. The size of influence on society is measured as to how much we are valued in a virtual world such as Social Media. Each person might not have an equal weight of voice, but instead, we are all reachable to each other and even authorities.
