10 Digits (2020)

Coding Art - created with MAX MSP

Idenitification / Digital Era / Phone number


This piece is created with MAX MSP, inspired by CV Dazzle by Adam Harvey. Harvey created various make-ups to "escape" from facial recognition.

Nowadays, something companies and Social Media recognize you as you are not your name nor face. Whenever you register or make any contract, you need phone number.
The 10 digit numbers assigned randomly by the cell phone company is the biggest identification of us.

The 10 digit numbers travel somewhere I have no ideas about, and exist as if they are me.
When you put your phone number, there will appear a filter based on your number on your face as if like your phone number hide and distort your face.
Who are we in this digital world?
